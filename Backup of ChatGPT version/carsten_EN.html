Game Rules

Carsten's Cube is a strategic game for two players. The objective is to form a row, column, or diagonal of four shapes that are either all the same or all different. Players take turns selecting a shape from the staging area and placing it on the playing field. The first player to form a complete row, column, or diagonal with the specified criteria wins.
